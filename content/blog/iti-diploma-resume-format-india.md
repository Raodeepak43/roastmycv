## ITI vs Diploma — resume me kya alag hai?

| | ITI (1–2 year trade) | Diploma (Polytechnic 3 year) |
|---|----------------------|------------------------------|
| Headline | "ITI Electrician — NCVT" | "Diploma Mechanical Engineer" |
| Focus | Trade tools, workshop, apprenticeship | Projects, CAD, plant exposure |
| Typical jobs | Technician, operator, apprentice | Junior engineer, supervisor trainee |
| Education line | ITI + trade + year | Diploma + branch + % |

## ITI resume format (one page)

**Header:** Name | Phone | City | Trade (e.g. Electrician)

**Objective:** "NCVT ITI Electrician seeking apprenticeship / technician role in manufacturing — immediate joiner."

**Technical skills:** Wiring, motor repair, safety PPE, tools (honest list)

**Education:**
- ITI — Trade, Institute, Year, % 
- 10th/12th — Board, Year

**Apprenticeship / Experience:** Company, role, months, machines worked on

**Certifications:** NCVT marksheet, optional driving license for field roles

## Diploma resume format (one page)

**Objective:** "Diploma Mechanical — seeking graduate engineer trainee / junior supervisor role."

**Education:** Diploma first (branch, college, CGPA/%), then 10th/12th

**Projects:** Mini project title, tools (AutoCAD, SolidWorks), outcome in one line

**Internship:** Plant name, department, 4–8 weeks, what you observed/did

**Skills:** Technical + soft — match JD keywords

## Common trades — objective examples

**Electrician:** "ITI Electrician, 6 months industrial apprenticeship — motor winding, panel wiring, safety compliant."

**COPA:** "COPA trade — MS Office, Tally basic, data entry 40 WPM, office assistant role."

**Fitter:** "Fitter ITI — lathe, drilling, assembly line exposure, factory shift ready."

**Diploma CS/IT:** "Diploma CS — Java basics, SQL, mini ERP project, junior developer trainee."

## Mistakes jo reject karwati hain

- Trade mention nahi kiya — HR filter miss
- "All trade work" vague likhna — specific tools likho
- Photo scan PDF — ATS fail
- Fake apprenticeship — verification hoti hai PSU me
- Email typo — phone primary rakho agar email rarely check karte ho
