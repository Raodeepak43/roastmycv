**BTech fresher resume mistakes** cost shortlists silently — TPO ne forward kar diya, par ATS ya recruiter ne 8 seconds mein reject kar diya. Yeh 10 errors Indian engineering campus cycles mein sabse common hain.

## 1. Generic objective line

*"To work in a dynamic organization..."* — delete. Replace with 2-line summary + target role.

## 2. CGPA chhupana ya galat jagah

CGPA eligible cutoff ke upar hai toh visible rakho. Footer mein mat daba ke rakho.

## 3. Projects bina tech stack ke

*"E-commerce website"* — kaunsi stack? tumhara contribution kya tha?

## 4. Skills = MS Word + Internet

Recruiter roast karega. Technical skills honest + project-linked rakho.

## 5. 3-page fresher CV

1 page enough for 0–1 YOE. [Campus placement checker](/tools/campus-placement-resume-checker) use karo.

## 6. Canva multi-column design

ATS parse fail. Single column PDF.

## 7. No GitHub for CS/IT

SDE pipeline mein red flag.

## 8. Copy-paste same resume har company ko

Keywords tweak karo — same file mass forward mat karo.

## 9. Spelling + tense inconsistency

Past internships = past tense bullets.
