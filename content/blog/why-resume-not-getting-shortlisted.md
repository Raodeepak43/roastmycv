**Why is my resume not getting shortlisted?** Because recruiters and ATS filter out most files in seconds — not because you have zero talent. The fix starts with diagnosing *which* filter is killing you.

## Reason 1: ATS never read your CV properly

Multi-column Canva layouts, icons, text boxes → parser drops content. [ATS India fresher guide](/blog/why-ats-rejects-resume-india-fresher).

## Reason 2: You look like everyone else

*"Hardworking, team player, quick learner"* — thousands of identical lines. No memorable project metric.

## Reason 3: Skills mismatch

Job wants SQL + Python; resume lists only *"programming"*. Keyword gap = auto reject.

## Reason 4: Eligibility before human review

CGPA, backlog, gap rules — form level reject.

## Reason 5: Weak top third

Recruiter never scrolls to page 2 if line 1–5 bore them.
