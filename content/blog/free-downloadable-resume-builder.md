## Resume Layout Free: What ATS Wants

**Free resume layout** does not mean decorative. It means:

- One column
- Standard headings: Education, Experience, Projects, Skills
- Black text, 10–11pt body font
- No tables, text boxes, or skill percentage bars
- Standard section order

Fancy **resume layout free** templates from Pinterest often fail when downloaded. Stick to ATS layouts in the [builder](/resume-builder).
