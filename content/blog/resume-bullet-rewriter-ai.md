"Worked on various projects" and "Responsible for team coordination" are resume killers. Recruiters want **metrics, action verbs, and outcomes**. The **Bullet Rewriter** takes your existing bullets and returns stronger versions grounded in what you actually did — without inventing fake numbers.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Bullet Rewriter** ✨ tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Bullet Rewriter?

Paste weak bullets from your CV. AI rewrites each with stronger verbs, clearer scope, and suggested metrics placeholders where you should add real data. Keeps facts from your CV — does not hallucinate employers or titles.

Access it from your dashboard: [Bullet Rewriter →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/cv-rewriter)

Freshers with empty-looking project sections and professionals whose bullets read like job descriptions.

## How to Use Bullet Rewriter

1. Copy 3–5 weak bullets from your experience or projects section.
2. Open **Bullet Rewriter** and paste them.
3. Replace [X%] placeholders with your real numbers.
4. Update your CV in the [free resume builder](/resume-builder).
