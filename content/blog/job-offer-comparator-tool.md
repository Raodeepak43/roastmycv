This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Offer Comparator** ⚖️ tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Offer Comparator?

Paste offer details (CTC breakdown, role, company, location, perks). AI compares on: compensation, growth trajectory, culture signals from JD language, red flags, and overall fit. Includes script to leverage competing offer.

Access it from your dashboard: [Offer Comparator →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/offer-compare)

Candidates with 2+ active offers in the same week — common in campus and hot tech markets.

## How to Use Offer Comparator

1. Include ESOPs, bonus, and WFH policy — not just base.
2. Discuss with someone who worked at each company if possible.
3. Use **Salary Script** to negotiate after choosing preferred offer.
