LinkedIn Writer fixes your About section. **LinkedIn Audit** reviews your **entire profile** — headline keyword density, experience bullets, skills order, banner, and recruiter discoverability — with a prioritized fix list.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **LinkedIn Audit** 🔍 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is LinkedIn Audit?

Paste your full LinkedIn profile text (or export from CV). AI audits each section, scores strengths/weaknesses, and lists top 5 fixes ranked by impact on recruiter search.

Access it from your dashboard: [LinkedIn Audit →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/linkedin-audit)

Anyone who gets profile views but no InMails, or who has not updated LinkedIn in 12+ months.

## How to Use LinkedIn Audit

1. Copy all sections from LinkedIn into the tool.
2. Fix headline and About first — highest ROI.
3. Add skills recruiters search for your role.
4. Try [LinkedIn Roast](/linkedin-roast) for brutal feedback.
