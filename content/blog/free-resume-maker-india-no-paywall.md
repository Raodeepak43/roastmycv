## When to Use Canva Anyway

Canva is fine for:

- Creative roles (design, content, marketing)
- Networking PDFs that skip ATS
- Portfolio-style CVs sent direct to hiring manager email

Canva is **risky** for:

- TCS/Infosys/Wipro campus drives
- Naukri/LinkedIn Easy Apply bulk applications
- Any employer using Taleo, Workday, or iCIMS
