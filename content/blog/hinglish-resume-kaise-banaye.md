**Hinglish resume kaise banaye** — yeh sawal har campus season pe WhatsApp groups mein ghoomta hai. Matlab English corporate template copy karna nahi; aisi CV jo Indian recruiter ko natural lage aur ATS bhi parse kar sake.

## Hinglish resume kya hota hai?

Hinglish resume = professional English structure + Indian context (CGPA, internships, Indian company names) + kabhi-kabhi Hinglish tone summary mein. Full Hindi CV MNC ATS ke liye risky hai; pure American template Indian fresher ke liye fake lagta hai. Sweet spot: **clean English bullets, desi proof points**.

## Step 1: Header sahi karo

- Full name, phone (+91), professional email (not `[email protected]`)
- LinkedIn + GitHub (tech roles)
- City, India — full address not needed

## Step 2: Summary — 2 lines max

Voice-search friendly: *"BTech CSE fresher with 2 full-stack projects and 3-month internship — targeting SDE / campus placement roles."*

Avoid: *"Hardworking, passionate, quick learner seeking opportunity in reputed organization."*

## Step 3: Education

Degree, college, CGPA (if >7 or company cutoff clear), year. Backlogs honestly handle karo — mat chhupao agar form mein declare karna hai.

## Step 4: Projects (fresher ka experience section)

Har project mein:
- Tech stack
- Tumhara role
- Outcome/number (*"Reduced load time 40%"*, *"500+ test users"*)

## Step 5: Skills — grouped

Languages | Frameworks | Tools | Databases — comma lists, honest level.

## Step 6: One page rule

Campus drives = 1 page default. [One-page resume guide](/blog/one-page-resume-guide) dekho.
