**Naukri resume tips** matter because Naukri is still India's largest job portal — but uploading a PDF and clicking "Apply" 200 times is not a strategy. Recruiters search Naukri's database; if your profile ranks low, you get **zero views** while competitors with keyword-matched headlines show up first.

No dedicated Naukri guide existed in our blog until now — this fills that gap.

## How Naukri Actually Ranks Your Profile

Recruiters search by:
- **Job title keywords** (exact match wins)
- **Skills** (Naukri skill tags)
- **Experience band** (fresher vs 2–5 years)
- **Location** and **notice period**
- **Profile freshness** (last updated date)
- **Resume completeness**

Your resume PDF is parsed — if layout breaks, keywords vanish.

## Top 10 Naukri Resume Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Headline: "Fresher seeking opportunity" | "B.Tech CSE Fresher | Java, Python | Open to SDE-1" |
| One generic CV for all roles | Tailor skills + summary per job family |
| Canva two-column PDF | Single-column ATS PDF |
| Profile 40% complete | Fill every section Naukri prompts |
| Resume not updated in 6 months | Refresh monthly during job hunt |
| Skills: MS Office only | Match JD keywords from target roles |
| No project proof | 2 projects with stack + outcome |
| Applying to 50 unrelated jobs | Low response rate hurts visibility |
| Photo on resume | Skip for IT/service MNCs |
| Not using Naukri FastForward keywords | Research role-specific terms |

## Naukri Resume Format Checklist

- **File:** PDF, text-selectable (highlight test in preview)
- **Length:** 1 page fresher, 2 max experienced
- **Sections:** Contact, Summary, Education, Skills, Experience/Projects
- **Keywords:** Copy phrases from 5 target Naukri job posts
- **Headline:** 80 chars, role-specific

## Naukri + Roast Workflow

1. Build ATS CV in [resume builder](/resume-builder)
