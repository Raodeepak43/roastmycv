LinkedIn Easy Apply is a black hole. A sharp **cold email** to a recruiter or hiring manager can skip the queue — if it is under 150 words, specific, and not "Dear Sir/Madam I hope this email finds you well." The **Cold Email** tool writes human outreach from your CV.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Cold Email** 📧 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Cold Email?

Provide CV, target company, role, and recruiter name if known. AI outputs: subject line (specific, not "Job Application"), 120-word body with one quantified achievement, and a 15-minute call ask. Sounds like a person, not a mail merge.

Access it from your dashboard: [Cold Email →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/cold-email)

Mid-level professionals, niche role seekers, and anyone targeting dream companies directly.

## How to Use Cold Email

1. Research the company — one real detail helps (product launch, funding, blog post).
2. Generate the email in **Cold Email**.
3. Send from a professional address, Tuesday–Thursday morning IST.
4. Follow up once after 5 business days using the **Follow-Up** tool.
