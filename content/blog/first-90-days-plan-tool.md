Starting a new job without a plan means reactive firefighting for three months. A **30-60-90 day plan** shows your manager you are strategic — and helps you earn trust fast. **First 90 Days** generates a role-specific plan from your CV and new job details.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **First 90 Days** 🚀 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is First 90 Days?

Enter CV, new title, company, and team context. AI outputs week-by-week plan: people to meet, skills to learn, quick wins for days 1–30, ownership targets for 60–90, and metrics to track.

Access it from your dashboard: [First 90 Days →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/ninety-days)

New joiners, especially lateral hires and first-time managers.

## How to Use First 90 Days

1. Share draft with manager in week 1 — align expectations.
2. Adjust plan after understanding real team priorities.
3. Review at day 30 and day 60.
