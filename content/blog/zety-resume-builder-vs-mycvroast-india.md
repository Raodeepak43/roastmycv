## What Is Zety Resume Builder?

Zety (zety.com) is a popular **online resume builder** with:

- Step-by-step section prompts ("experience", "education", "skills")
- Dozens of visual templates
- Cover letter builder and career blog content
- Strong brand recognition — hence searches like **zety resume**, **zety cv**, and **zety resume builder free**

Zety's free experience typically lets you **create** your resume in the browser. When you click download, you are often pushed toward a subscription or short trial. That model works for Zety's business, but it frustrates students who expected "free" to mean "free PDF."

## Where Zety Falls Short for India

### 1. Download paywall

Indian freshers frequently search **build your resume for free** or **create resume online free**. Zety's funnel builds hope, then charges at export. MyCVRoast's [free resume builder](/resume-builder) is designed so you can export without that surprise.

### 2. ATS parsing risk

Large Indian employers run CVs through ATS before a human reads them. Templates with:

- Two-column layouts
- Skill bars and icons
- Text inside tables or text boxes

…can scramble when parsed. Zety has clean options, but not all templates are ATS-safe. Read our [ATS-friendly resume guide](/blog/ats-friendly-resume) before picking any template.

### 3. No roast-style feedback

Zety helps you **write** a resume. It does not tell you, in plain language, why a recruiter would skip your bullet points in six seconds. MyCVRoast's [free AI resume roast](/) quotes your actual CV and scores it — a step Zety users often still need from Reddit, ChatGPT, or friends.

Campus placement, CGPA rules, service-based company formats, and Hinglish tone matter in India. Zety is built for a global audience.
