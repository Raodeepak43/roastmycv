**12th pass BPO resume format** — call centre aur customer support India me sabse zyada **12th pass + fluent Hindi/English** hiring hoti hai. Voice process, non-voice, chat support, email support — sab me **simple one-page resume** chahiye: clear phone, typing speed (honest), languages, aur night shift willingness.

## BPO resume format — sections (order)

1. **Contact** — Name, phone (double-check), city, email optional
2. **Objective** — Specific: "Voice process / customer support executive"
3. **Education** — 12th board, year, % (10th bhi likh sakte ho)
4. **Skills** — Languages, typing WPM, MS Office, listening/speaking
5. **Experience** — Retail, internship, previous BPO (honest dates)
6. **Additional** — Night shift OK, immediate joiner, references

## Sample objective lines (12th pass BPO)

**Voice process:**
"12th pass (HBSE 2024), fluent Hindi & English, clear accent — seeking voice-based customer support role. Open to night shifts. Immediate joiner."

**Non-voice / chat:**
"12th pass with 38 WPM typing, MS Excel basic — non-voice email/chat support role. Rotational shifts acceptable."

**Avoid:**
"Need job urgently any shift any salary" — recruiter skip karta hai.

## Skills BPO recruiters actually filter on

| Skill | Voice BPO | Non-voice BPO |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| Hindi + English fluency | Critical | Important |
| Neutral / clear accent | Critical | Less |
| Typing WPM | Nice to have | Critical (35+) |
| MS Office | Basic | Excel often tested |
| Previous call centre exp | Plus | Plus |
| Night shift availability | Often required | Often required |

## Experience — agar nahi hai

Likho jo sach ho:

- Retail billing / mall counter — customer handling
- Tele-caller internship (even 1 month)
- Family shop — phone orders, complaints
- Computer course — mention institute

Example: "6 months retail billing — handled cash + customer queries, evening shift."

## BPO walk-in resume tips (India)

- **One page** black & white print + 2 copies
- Phone number **pen se mat overwrite** PDF pe
- Photo alag envelope me (if consultancy asks)
- Shift preference clearly likho
- Fake experience mat likho — background verification hoti hai larger BPOs me

## Voice vs non-voice — resume farq

**Voice:** Languages, accent clarity, patience, previous phone-facing role zyada highlight karo. Typing secondary.

**Non-voice:** Typing speed, email grammar, Excel — objective me "non-voice" explicitly likho.
