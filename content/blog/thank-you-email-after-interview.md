Sending a thank-you email within 24 hours puts you ahead of 70% of candidates who never follow up. It is not grovelling — it is professional closure plus one more chance to reinforce why you fit. The **Thank You Email** tool writes it from your CV and interview notes.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Thank You Email** ✉️ tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Thank You Email?

Paste CV, role, interviewer name, and 2–3 things discussed. AI writes a concise thank-you: references specific conversation points, ties one CV achievement to the role, and closes warmly. No essay length.

Access it from your dashboard: [Thank You Email →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/thank-you)

Anyone who completed a phone screen, technical round, or final HR round.

## How to Use Thank You Email

1. Send within 24 hours of the interview.
2. Personalise one line the AI cannot know (inside joke, shared interest).
3. One email to all panelists BCC if needed — or separate short notes.
