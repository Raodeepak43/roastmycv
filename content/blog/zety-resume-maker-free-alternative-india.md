## Zety resume maker — what you get

Zety (zety.com) offers:

- Step-by-step resume sections (experience, education, skills)
- Many visual templates
- Cover letter builder
- Strong brand — hence searches like **zety resume maker**, **zety cv**, **zety free resume**

The friction point: **free build, paid download**. Many students complete the entire CV, click export, and discover PDF requires subscription. That is not "free" in practice.

## Why Zety templates fail ATS in India

Indian employers (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Amazon India, Naukri bulk apply) use **Applicant Tracking Systems**. Templates with:

- Two columns
- Skill bars and graphics
- Icons instead of text headings

…can scramble when parsed. Your "beautiful" CV becomes unreadable garbage in the database.
