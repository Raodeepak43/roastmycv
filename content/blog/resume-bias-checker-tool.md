Indian CVs often include date of birth, photo, marital status, and religion — all of which can trigger **unconscious bias** and get you filtered before skills are read. Abroad, some of these are illegal to ask. **Bias Detector** flags risky content and suggests neutral alternatives.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Bias Detector** 🕵️ tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Bias Detector?

Paste CV and target country. AI lists bias triggers: demographic hints, gendered language, age signals, caste/religion references, and photo recommendations. Suggests rewrites that keep facts without triggering filters.

Access it from your dashboard: [Bias Detector →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/bias-check)

Women returning to workforce, older candidates, and anyone applying internationally from India.

## How to Use Bias Detector

1. Remove photo for US/UK/EU applications.
2. Replace "single/married" with nothing.
3. Use **CV Localiser** for country-specific rules.
