## What "Free Resume Help" Should Include

Real help is not just a blank template. You need:

1. **Structure** — what sections, in what order
2. **Content** — what to write when you have no experience
3. **Feedback** — what is weak before you apply
4. **Export** — a PDF that opens correctly on Naukri and company portals

If a tool charges at download or gives vague "looks good" feedback, it is not real help.

## Help Making a Resume for Free: Common Mistakes

- **Canva-first design** — pretty, ATS-broken. See [Canva vs builder](/blog/canva-resume-vs-builder)
- **Copying senior's 2-page CV** — freshers need one page, projects forward
- **Paying before trying free tools** — exhaust free roast + builder first
- **No second opinion** — always get feedback before mass applying
- **Wrong document** — biodata vs resume confusion. See [biodata maker guide](/blog/biodata-maker-for-job-india)

## When Paid Resume Help Makes Sense

Consider paid help only if:

- You are 10+ years experienced switching industries
- You need executive branding (C-suite, consulting)
- You are applying abroad with strict format rules

For **Indian freshers and 0–3 years experience**, free AI + guides + TPO review is enough.
