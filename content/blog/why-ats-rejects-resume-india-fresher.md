**Why does ATS reject your resume in India?** Applicant Tracking Systems score and filter CVs before recruiters open them — especially in high-volume fresher hiring (IT services, product campus drives, Naukri bulk apply).

## Parse failure (invisible reject)

ATS extracts text from PDF. Columns, icons, skill bars as graphics → **empty sections** in database.

## Keyword gap

Job post: *"Java, SQL, REST"*. Your CV: *"programming languages"*. No match → filtered out.

## Wrong section labels

Creative headings (*"My Journey"*) confuse parsers. Use standard: Education, Projects, Skills, Experience.

## Header/footer contact info

Some systems skip margins — phone in footer never stored.

## Fresher-specific: no searchable skills

Empty project descriptions = nothing to rank.
