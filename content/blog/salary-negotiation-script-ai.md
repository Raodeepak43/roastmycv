## What Is Salary Script?

Enter your offer details, role, location, and any competing offer. AI produces: opening response to the first number, counter-offer framing with data points, scripts for "budget is fixed," and email follow-up text. Calibrated for Indian salary conversations (CTC, ESOPs, joining bonus).

## How to Use Salary Script

1. Research market range on AmbitionBox / Glassdoor before the call.
2. Generate scripts in **Salary Script**.
3. Practice out loud — negotiate tone, not aggression.
4. Use **Offer Comparator** if you have multiple offers.
