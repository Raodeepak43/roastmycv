Your Indian CV with photo, 10th marks, and "father's name" will get rejected in the US and UK in seconds. **CV Localiser** adapts your content to target country conventions — not just spelling, but what to remove, add, and reorder.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **CV Localiser** 🌍 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is CV Localiser?

Paste CV and select target country (US, UK, Canada, Germany, UAE, etc.). AI explains format rules, flags inappropriate content, suggests visa/status line if relevant, and outputs a localised CV draft.

Access it from your dashboard: [CV Localiser →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/cv-localise)

Indian professionals applying abroad or to MNC regional offices.

## How to Use CV Localiser

1. Remove photo and personal details not used abroad.
2. Convert to 1–2 pages max for US/UK.
3. Run **JD Match** on each international application separately.
