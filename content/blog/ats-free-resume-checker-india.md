## What "ATS Free" Should Mean

Watch for traps:

- **Scan free, fix paid** — you see problems but not solutions
- **One scan only** — useless for 50 campus applications
- **No India context** — flags CGPA section as "unusual"

Real free value = score + actionable fixes + no credit card.

## ATS Rules Indian Freshers Miss

1. **No Canva columns** — parsers scramble text order
2. **Standard section headings** — "Experience" not "My Journey"
3. **Keywords from JD** — copy exact skill phrases
4. **One page** for campus — unless TPO says otherwise
5. **PDF export** — not image-based PDFs
