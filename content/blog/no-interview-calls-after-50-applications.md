**Applied to 50+ jobs and still no calls?** You're not alone — and it's usually not *only* the job market. One broken resume multiplied by 50 applications still equals zero interviews.

## The spray-and-pray trap

Same PDF everywhere → low keyword match everywhere. Volume without tailoring feels productive; ATS sees mismatch.

## Your resume is the bottleneck

Before applying #51, run [free resume roast India](/tools/free-resume-roast-india). If score is under 6/10, fix first.

## LinkedIn doesn't match your CV

Recruiters check both. Headline still *"Student at XYZ"* while applying SDE roles?

## Wrong channels only

Only Easy Apply, no referrals, no company career pages, no alumni network.

## Timing and role level

Applying senior roles as fresher = silence.

## Diagnostic checklist

- [ ] Single-column ATS PDF  
- [ ] Summary mentions target role  
- [ ] 2 projects with numbers  
- [ ] Last 10 applies had tweaked keywords  
- [ ] LinkedIn updated this week  
- [ ] [Roast score](/) improved vs first attempt  

## Next step

Stop applying until top 3 resume fixes done. Then 10 **tailored** applies > 50 generic.
