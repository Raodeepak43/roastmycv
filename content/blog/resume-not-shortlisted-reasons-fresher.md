**Resume not getting shortlisted** as a fresher usually is not one big failure — it is 3–4 small signals recruiters filter on in the first pass.

## CGPA and eligibility mismatch

Many drives have hard cutoffs (e.g. 60%, 65%, no active backlog). Form reject before human read.

## ATS never saw your skills

PDF columns, icons, tables → parser drops keywords. [Why ATS rejects in India](/blog/why-ats-rejects-resume-india-fresher) guide padho.

## Generic CV = zero memory

Recruiter saw 400 CVs that day. *"Team player, quick learner"* wali CV blend ho jati hai.

## No project proof

Fresher without internship still needs 2 solid projects with metrics.

## Wrong role targeting

Same resume for QA, data analyst, and full-stack — sab mein weak match.

## Email / phone typo

Happens more than you think. Double-check header.
