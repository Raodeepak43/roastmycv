## What Resume Roast AI Actually Does

A proper **AI resume roast**:

1. Parses your PDF or text (not just spell-check)
2. Scores your CV /10 with a one-line verdict
3. Quotes your weakest bullets verbatim
4. Lists 3–5 actionable fixes
5. Optional tone: clean → savage, including Hinglish

Generic "AI resume review" gives you: *"Add more action verbs."*  
**Resume roast AI** gives you: *"Bhai, 'hardworking team player' objective — yeh 2010 ka template hai."*
