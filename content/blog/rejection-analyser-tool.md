One rejection might be bad luck. Ten rejections with zero interviews means a **pattern** — usually a missing skill, wrong title targeting, or CV keyword gap that repeats across every JD. **Rejection Analyser** compares your CV to multiple JDs you failed at and finds the common root cause.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Rejection Analyser** 🔎 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Rejection Analyser?

Paste CV + 3–5 job descriptions you applied to but did not get. AI identifies skills/keywords that appear in multiple JDs but are missing or weak in your CV. One primary root cause + quick fixes.

Access it from your dashboard: [Rejection Analyser →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/rejection-analyser)

Candidates with 20+ applications and fewer than 3 interview calls.

## How to Use Rejection Analyser

1. Save JDs when you apply — build a rejection library.
2. Fix the #1 pattern before applying to 50 more roles.
3. Re-run **JD Match** on your next target role.
