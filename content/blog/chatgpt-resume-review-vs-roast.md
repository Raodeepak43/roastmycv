## ChatGPT Resume Review — How People Use It

Typical flow:

1. Copy-paste resume text into ChatGPT
2. Ask "review my resume"
3. Get polite bullet list: "add metrics", "use action verbs"
4. Feel good, apply anyway, no callbacks

**Problem:** No score, no quoted weak lines, no Hinglish reality check.

## When ChatGPT Wins

Use ChatGPT when you need:

- Custom cover letter for one company
- Role-specific bullet rewrite with long context
- Interview answer practice (open-ended)
