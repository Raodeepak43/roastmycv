Most cover letters are copy-paste disasters. Hiring managers spot generic letters in one line. A good cover letter connects **your CV achievements** to **this specific job** — and the **Cover Letter** tool does that automatically.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Cover Letter Generator** 📝 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Cover Letter Generator?

Paste your CV and job description. Choose tone (professional, confident, or concise). AI writes a ~250-word letter with: company-specific opening, 2–3 CV achievements mapped to JD requirements, and a clear call to action. No fabricated experience.

Access it from your dashboard: [Cover Letter Generator →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/cover-letter)

Roles where cover letters are expected — consulting, marketing, government, and senior individual contributor roles.

## How to Use Cover Letter Generator

1. Run **JD Match** first if you have not tailored your CV yet.
2. Open **Cover Letter**, paste CV + JD + company name.
3. Edit the output — add one personal detail the AI cannot know.
4. Save as PDF or paste into the application portal.
