Your resume is for jobs. Freelance platforms need a **different pitch** — outcome-focused bio, service packages, and keywords clients search. **Freelancer Profile** converts your CV into platform-ready copy for Upwork, Fiverr, and LinkedIn Services.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Freelancer Profile** 💼 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Freelancer Profile?

Paste CV and select platform + service niche. AI writes: headline, bio, 3 service descriptions, portfolio bullet summaries, and suggested hourly rate range for Indian market context.

Access it from your dashboard: [Freelancer Profile →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/freelancer-profile)
