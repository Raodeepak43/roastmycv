Stuck as "Software Engineer" with no idea if you should go toward PM, DevOps, or engineering manager? **Career Paths** maps realistic next steps from your actual CV — not generic "learn leadership" advice.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Career Paths** 🗺️ tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Career Paths?

Upload CV and optional interests. AI suggests 3–4 career paths with: typical next titles, skills gap per path, 12–24 month roadmap, and Indian market context (which paths hire more at your level).

Access it from your dashboard: [Career Paths →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/career-path)

2–7 year professionals feeling plateaued or considering a pivot.

## How to Use Career Paths

1. Pick one path — do not chase all four.
2. Run **Skills Gap** for the chosen path.
3. Update LinkedIn headline to match direction.

## Free vs Pro Access

Pro plan feature. Pro users get unlimited access to all dashboard AI tools including Career Paths. See [pricing plans](/plans) for details.

## Tips for Better Results

- **Use fresh CV text** — run a [free resume roast](/) first to fix obvious issues.
- **Be specific** — role titles, company names, and dates improve output quality.
- **Edit the AI draft** — treat output as a strong first draft, not final copy.
- **Combine tools** — pair Career Paths with other MyCVRoast tools in your workflow.
