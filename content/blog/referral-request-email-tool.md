Referrals get 4× more responses than cold applications. But "Can you refer me?" to someone you met once at a webinar feels cringe. **Referral Ask** writes messages that make it **easy for them to say yes** — short, specific, with your CV highlights attached in text.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Referral Ask** 🤝 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Referral Ask?

Enter connection context (alumni, ex-colleague, LinkedIn contact), target role, company, and CV summary. AI drafts LinkedIn DM and email versions with clear ask and low friction for the referrer.

Access it from your dashboard: [Referral Ask →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/referral)

Candidates targeting specific companies where they have 1st or 2nd degree connections.

## How to Use Referral Ask

1. Warm up the connection first — comment on their post or congratulate a work anniversary.
2. Send referral ask with JD link and 2-line CV summary.
3. Make it forwardable — they should copy-paste to internal portal.
