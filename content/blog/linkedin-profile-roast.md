Your **LinkedIn profile** is your digital first impression — and most profiles are painfully generic. "Passionate professional | Team player | Open to opportunities" tells recruiters nothing. They scroll past in two seconds.

That's why we built **[LinkedIn Roast](/linkedin-roast)** — the same brutally honest AI energy as our CV roast, but tuned for LinkedIn profiles.

## What LinkedIn Roast Checks

Paste your full LinkedIn profile text (Ctrl+A on your profile page, then paste). Our AI scores you on:

- **Headline** — Is it keyword-rich or just your job title from 2019?
- **About** — Does it sell you or read like a Wikipedia stub?
- **Experience** — Bullet points or vague paragraphs nobody reads?
- **Skills** — Endorsed for "Microsoft Office" and nothing else?
- **Activity** — Ghost account or actually visible to recruiters?

You get an **overall score out of 10**, section scores, a roast in your chosen language (Hinglish included), and **3 specific fixes** you can apply today.

## Why Roast Before You Optimize

Generic LinkedIn advice says "add keywords" and "be professional." That doesn't help when your About section literally says "I am a hard working individual looking for challenging opportunities."

A roast tells you **exactly** what's cringe, what's missing, and what recruiters are silently judging. It's the feedback you'd never get from a connection request.
