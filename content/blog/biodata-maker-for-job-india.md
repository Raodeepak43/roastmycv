## Biodata vs Resume vs CV in India

| Document | Typical use | Personal details | Best for |
|----------|-------------|------------------|----------|
| **Biodata** | Govt, PSU, banking, teaching forms | Often more (DOB, parents' name) | Form-based hiring |
| **Resume** | Private companies, startups, IT | Minimal — contact + LinkedIn | TCS, Infosys, startups |
| **CV** | Academia, research, abroad | Moderate | PhD, research roles |

If the employer says "upload resume on Naukri/LinkedIn" — they want a **resume**, not a marriage biodata. If the form has fields for father's name and marital status — they want **biodata**.

## What a Job Biodata Should Include

A standard **job biodata** in India (1–2 pages) usually has:

1. **Personal information** — full name, date of birth, gender (if asked), address, phone, email
2. **Objective** — one line on the role you want
3. **Educational qualifications** — 10th, 12th, degree, CGPA, year
4. **Work experience** — company, role, dates, 2–3 bullets (if any)
5. **Skills** — relevant to the job
6. **Languages known**
7. **Declaration** — "I hereby declare…" with place, date, signature line

**Do not** add photo unless the employer asks. Many private companies discourage it for bias reasons.

## Biodata Format for Freshers (No Experience)

Freshers should emphasize:

- **Education** — degree, CGPA, relevant coursework
- **Projects** — 2–3 with tech stack and outcome
- **Internships / training** — even 1–2 weeks counts
- **Skills** — match the job description keywords

## Biodata for Government & Banking Jobs

Government and PSU applications often use:

- Prescribed application forms (fill PDF online)
- Specific biodata annexures

**Tip:** Copy the exact section order from the official notification. Do not use a creative template — clerks scan for missing fields.

For private banking (HDFC, ICICI, etc.), use a **professional resume** unless the portal says biodata.

- Using marriage biodata layout for IT jobs

## When to Use Resume Instead of Biodata

Use a modern **resume** or **CV** for:

- Campus placement and off-campus IT drives
- Startup and product company applications
- LinkedIn and Naukri Easy Apply
- Remote jobs India — see [remote jobs guide](/blog/remote-jobs-india-guide)

Use **biodata** when:

- The application form explicitly asks for it
- Government, PSU, or traditional sector notifications
- School or college admin jobs with prescribed format
