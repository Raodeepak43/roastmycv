Generic interview question lists on Google do not know you had a 8-month gap, switched from mechanical to IT, or led a college fest with 500 attendees. **Interview Prep** reads your CV and generates questions interviewers will ask **you** — with answer frameworks using your real experience.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Interview Prep** 🎤 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Interview Prep?

Upload CV + target role. AI groups 10–12 likely questions into: Behavioural, Technical, CV-specific, and Situational. Each includes why they ask it and a STAR-style answer skeleton from your bullets.

Access it from your dashboard: [Interview Prep →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/interview-prep)

Campus placement, off-campus, and lateral hire candidates at any level.

## How to Use Interview Prep

1. Run prep 3–5 days before the interview.
2. Write bullet answers — do not memorise word-for-word.
3. Do a **Mock Interview** session for practice under pressure.
4. After the interview, run **Interview Debrief**.

## Tips for Better Results

- **Use fresh CV text** — run a [free resume roast](/) first to fix obvious issues.

- **Edit the AI draft** — treat output as a strong first draft, not final copy.
