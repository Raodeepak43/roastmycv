"Tell me about yourself" is not your life story. It is a **30-second pitch**: who you are, what you do best, and what you want next. The **Elevator Pitch** tool compresses your CV into spoken intro versions for interviews, campus fairs, and LinkedIn cold messages.

This guide explains how the MyCVRoast **Elevator Pitch** 🎤 tool works, who it is for, and how to get the best results in India's 2026 job market.

## What Is Elevator Pitch?

Paste CV and target audience (recruiter, technical panel, networking event). AI outputs 30-second and 60-second versions plus a one-line hook for DMs.

Access it from your dashboard: [Elevator Pitch →](https://www.mycvroast.in/dashboard/tools/elevator-pitch)

Freshers, career switchers, and anyone who rambles when asked to introduce themselves.

## How to Use Elevator Pitch

1. Memorise structure, not script word-for-word.
2. Practice with a timer — aim for 25–35 seconds.
3. Adjust tone for campus vs. corporate networking.
