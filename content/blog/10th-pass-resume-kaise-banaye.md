## 10th pass vs 12th pass resume — farq kya hai?

| Point | 10th pass | 12th pass |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Education line | "Matriculation (10th), Board, Year, %" | "12th (Science/Commerce/Arts), Board, Year, %" |
| Roles | Helper, peon, delivery, basic BPO | BPO executive, retail senior, apprenticeship |
| Skills focus | Basic English/Hindi, physical stamina, honesty | Typing WPM, MS Office, customer handling |

## Basic format (copy karo, adapt karo)

**Header:** Naam | Phone | City (email optional agar nahi use karte)

**Objective:** Ek specific job — "Retail billing assistant" ya "BPO voice process — Hindi/English"

**Education:** 10th pass, board name, year, percentage

**Skills:** MS Office basic, typing speed (honest), local language, two-wheeler license agar field job

**Experience:** Part-time, family business, internship — dates + task

**References:** "Available on request" (optional)

## Objective lines jo kaam karti hain

✅ "10th pass, Hindi/English communication, 4 months retail billing — customer-facing role immediately available."

❌ "Hardworking boy need any job urgently" — vague, reject.

## Experience nahi hai to kya likhein

- Father/mother shop me billing ya stock help — "6 months evening hours"
- Tuition padhaya primary students — "3 students, 1 year"
- Computer course (DCA/BCC) — institute name + certificate year
- NSS / school volunteer / sports team — role + duration

Jhoot mat likho — training period me pakde jaoge.
